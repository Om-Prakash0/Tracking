✨ The Solution

Introducing SafeRoute — Complete School Transport Safety

A powerful platform that works on the driver's existing smartphone — zero hardware investment, full peace of mind.

Live GPS Bus Tracking

Real-time bus location on a live map. Parents see exactly where the bus is, every second of the journey.

Real-Time ETA

Accurate estimated arrival times so parents know exactly when to expect the bus — no more waiting blindly.

Student Boarding Notifications

Instant push notifications when your child boards or exits the bus, with timestamp and location.

Two-Way Attendance

Conductor marks attendance digitally, parents confirm receipt. Both sides stay informed and accountable.

SOS Emergency Alerts

One-tap SOS from driver or conductor instantly alerts school admin, parents, and emergency contacts.

Route Deviation Alerts

Automatic alerts if the bus goes off its designated route — with real-time location shared instantly.

Alternate Pickup Verification

Monthly Feedback System

Structured monthly feedback from parents to schools helps continuously improve transport quality.

Offline Data Sync

Attendance and trip data sync automatically when connectivity is restored — no data loss in poor network areas.
